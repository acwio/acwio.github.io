Overview

Human-AI Interaction is an interdisciplinary area of computing concerned with interactivity in intelligent systems. This course serves as an introduction to the design and development of human-centered AI systems. Topics covered include computer interaction, agency and intiative, trust and transparency, human augmentation and amplification, and fairness and interpretability.
